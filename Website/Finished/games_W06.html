Games

Well, what can I say only point towards the amount of hours I've played games on steam and cry at my sad little life.... Oh well... Games are great!! This shall just be a list and description of some of the stuff I'm playing at the moment with a few videos and maybe some screen shots along the way, enjoy!

Cities: Skylines

Cities: Skylines is a modern take on the classic city simulation. And after the absolute flop that was the latest Sim City, gamers everywhere rejoiced. It started on steam early access and has made its way to a full release. Not only that but its getting brilliant reviews and has sold a record number of copies for its publisher Paradox. Here is a short video to show you what it's like.

I really enjoy this game. Building a huge city and dealing with the issues like traffic and then being able to watch all your little people walk and drive around is amazing. They have also promised to keep releasing more and more content, some of it for free and then larger expansion packs that will be relatively cheap. Already they added a few features requested by fans in only a week after launch. Here are a few screenshots I took while playing.

Guild Wars 2

Guild Wars 2 is a massively multiplayer online role-playing game developed by ArenaNet and published by NCsoft. Set in the fantasy world of Tyria, the game follows the re-emergence of Destiny's Edge, a disbanded guild dedicated to fighting the Elder Dragons, a Lovecraftian species that has seized control of Tyria in the time since the original Guild Wars. The game takes place in a persistent world with a story that progresses in instanced environments. I picked it up with a few friends and we are playing through together. Once again I love this game. It has a nice mix of action combat along with the whole MMO thing which make it stand out from the crowd.

For a proper run down on what its like here is PC Gamers review.
